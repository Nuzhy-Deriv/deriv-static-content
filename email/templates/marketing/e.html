How to set up your Deriv
MT5 account


Hello!

We hope you’ve enjoyed exploring our demo account on the MT5 app.

Now that you’ve had a taste of the Deriv experience, why not join over 300,000 people trading the world’s financial markets with real Deriv MetaTrader 5 accounts?

You can choose from any of the three MT5 account types that we offer:

  • Synthetic account to trade volatility indices 24/7
  • Financial account to trade forex, cryptos, & commodities
  • Financial STP account to trade larger volumes of your favourite asset

    • Here’s how to set up your account in minutes:

Sign up for a Deriv account and verify your email ID.

Once you’ve verified your email, click the Start here button under Margin (MetaTrader 5).

Click on the Real account tab and proceed to create your Deriv currency account (fiat or crypto). 

Choose your account currency and complete the setup.

Once you’ve created your Deriv currency account, follow these additional steps to add the type of MT5 account that you want.

Congratulations! Your Deriv MT5 account is ready. If you’ve any questions, feel free to contact us via the live chat on our website.
